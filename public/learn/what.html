What Can We Recycle?

(Source: http://www.getitrightbinnight.vic.gov.au/what-can-i-recycle)

Glass

  • Glass bottles and jars

Plastic

  • Know your plastics.

  • If you’re unsure where your particular type of plastic should go – do the ‘scrunch test’:

    Hard plastic packaging that keeps its structure or bounces back when you try squashing it goes into your recycle bin.

    Soft plastics, such as plastic bags, bread bags, freezer bags and more cannot be recycled via your regular kerbside bin.

Paper

  • Books
  • Boxes/Cardboard
  • Brochures
  • Envelopes
  • Greeting cards
  • Junk mail

Metal

  • Aerosol spray cans (e.g deodorant, hair spray, air freshener)
  • Cans (tin, steel, aluminium)
  • Cake trays (foil)

Non-Recyclable

Do not put the following items in your recycling bin:

  • Plastic bags
  • Soft plastics
  • CDs, DVDs and VCR tapes
  • Expanded polystyrene foam used in take-away coffee cups or food packaging
  • Ceramics
  • Food scraps
  • Glass from broken glasses, windows or mirrows
  • Syringes and needles
